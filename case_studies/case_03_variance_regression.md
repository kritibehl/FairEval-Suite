# Case Study 03 — Variance Regression

## Failure class
Repeated-run instability

## Scenario
A model produces inconsistent behavior across repeated evaluation runs.

## Why score-only evals miss it
Single-run scores hide instability and overstate release confidence.

## FairEval signal
- score variance
- pass-rate variance
- repeated-run benchmark summary

## Release impact
High variance increases operational risk because behavior may change across identical or similar prompts.
